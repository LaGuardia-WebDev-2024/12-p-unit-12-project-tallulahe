My Top 3 Memories of This Year

  1. Getting Into College

    I still remember the exact moment and overpowering sense of relief when getting into my dream school. I never thought I would be able to do it, so I was shocked and beyond grateful. This is what I had been working up to throughout highschool, and what I had been in immense stress about. So this was my top memory.

  2. Senior Memories

    Senior year was such a blast! I enjoyed making new memories, which will be my final memories of highschool! From swim team, to spirit week, to coding classes! I enjoyed every minute! Well most minutes... take a good few off.

  3. Hiking Trip In The Rockies!

    I did a 10 day hiking trip through the Rockies with Outward Bound. Some of, if not the most beautiful views I have every seen were on this trip. We (a group of 12 teenagers) hiked all day, set up tarps at night. I had a few scary encounters, given we had to sleep solo one night, but the stars made up for everything!
